Our Story

Precision. Purpose. Personal.

What began as an overnight decision in 2022 has grown into a successful and steadily expanding boutique law firm. Led by director Mignon de Jager Hattingh, a seasoned attorney specialising in commercial law and contracts, the firm is built on hard work, dedication, and a commitment to achieving the best possible outcomes for its clients. The DJH team provides practical, tailored legal advice, recognising that every client and every matter is unique.

Commercial Law

Insolvency Law

Wills

Correspondent Work

Debt Collection

Efficient recovery of outstanding debts through professional, legally compliant processes.

The People

Our Professionals

MH

Mignon de Jager Hattingh

Director · Notary

LLB, NWU

mignon@djhattorneys.co.za

Mignon founded the firm in 2022 with a passion for providing practical, detail-oriented legal services. She obtained her Bachelor of Laws degree from the North-West University in 2017. After completing her articles, she was admitted as an attorney and later went on to qualify as a notary. Her commitment to excellence was recognised when she achieved the highest mark in the province in the Notarial Practice examination. Mignon specialises in commercial law and has experience in a wide range of commercial matters, particularly in the mining industry. She is highly experienced in drafting, reviewing and negotiating contracts, providing clients with practical legal solutions. She believes in building long-term relationships with clients and is committed to providing each client with personal attention and practical legal solutions.

AL

Angelique Laas

Senior Associate

LLB, UNISA

angelique@djhattorneys.co.za

Angelique, a senior attorney at the firm, obtained her LLB degree from the University of South Africa in 2013 and was admitted as an attorney in 2015. She joined the firm in December 2025, and since then, she has become an invaluable member of the team. Angelique specialises in civil litigation across a broad range of legal fields. Her extensive knowledge of civil procedure and substantive law, coupled with her strategic and meticulous approach to litigation, makes her a trusted advisor and a strong advocate for clients involved in legal disputes.

Boitumelo Mphela

boitumelo@djhattorneys.co.za

Boitumelo's journey with the firm began in 2024 when she joined the team as a candidate attorney.
